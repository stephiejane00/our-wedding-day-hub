Refund Policy

Full policy details

Effective Date: 20 March 2026

1. Overview

This Refund Policy applies to vendor subscriptions purchased through Our Wedding Day Hub. It explains what fees are refundable, under what conditions a refund may be granted, and how to request one.

2. Setup Fee

The one-off vendor account setup fee is non-refundable in all circumstances.

This fee covers the cost of creating and activating your vendor account, setting up your profile space, and onboarding your business to the platform. This work is performed at the time of account creation regardless of whether you continue using the platform, and so this fee cannot be returned once paid.

3. Subscription Fees — 60 Day Guarantee

Vendor subscription fees are eligible for a refund under our 60 day satisfaction guarantee:

If you request a refund within the first 60 days of your subscription start date, we will refund up to 2 months of subscription fees.

This refund covers subscription payments only and does not apply to the one-off setup fee.

To be eligible, your refund request must be submitted within 60 days of your subscription start date.

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method where possible. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

4. Subscription Fees After 60 Days

Subscription fees paid outside of the 60 day guarantee window are generally non-refundable. If you cancel your subscription after 60 days, your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period and will not renew after that point.

We do not offer partial refunds for unused time within a billing period unless required by applicable law.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us through the website contact form within 60 days of your subscription start date. Please include:

  • your business name and the email address used to create your account;
  • the date you signed up; and
  • a brief note confirming you are requesting a refund under the 60 day guarantee.

We will review your request and respond as promptly as possible. Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method where available.

6. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time by contacting us. Cancellation means your subscription will not renew at the end of the current billing cycle. Your listing will remain active until the end of the period already paid for.

7. Australian Consumer Law

Nothing in this Refund Policy limits or excludes any rights you may have under the Australian Consumer Law or other applicable legislation where those rights cannot be excluded.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page and will apply going forward. We encourage you to review it periodically.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or would like to request a refund, please get in touch through the contact page on the website.
