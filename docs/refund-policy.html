Refund Policy

This policy outlines how refunds are handled for Our Wedding Day Hub services, including vendor setup fees and subscription payments.

We want our policies to feel clear, fair, and easy to understand before you make a payment.

Effective Date: 20 March 2026

1. Overview

This Refund Policy applies to all payments made to Our Wedding Day Hub, including vendor setup fees and subscription fees. By making a payment, you agree to this policy.

2. Vendor Setup Fee

The one-time setup fee, such as the $50 vendor setup fee, covers the creation and activation of your vendor account, access to the platform, and the ability to build and manage your listing.

The account setup fee is non-refundable once payment has been made, including if:

  • you change your mind;
  • you decide not to use the platform;
  • you do not complete your profile; or
  • you do not receive enquiries or bookings.

3. Subscription Fees

If your plan includes a recurring subscription, you will be billed according to your selected plan.

Vendors may cancel their subscription at any time.

If a vendor chooses to cancel, we offer a refund of up to 60 days, or 2 months, of subscription fees.

This refund applies to subscription fees only and does not apply to the one-time account setup fee.

Any refund will be processed back to the original payment method where possible.

4. Exceptional Circumstances

In rare cases, we may also consider an additional refund or adjustment at our discretion, including:

  • duplicate payments;
  • technical errors that prevent access to the platform; or
  • other situations we deem reasonable.

Any additional approved refunds will be assessed on a case-by-case basis and are not guaranteed.

5. No Guarantee of Results

Our Wedding Day Hub is a platform designed to increase visibility and connection opportunities. We do not guarantee enquiries, leads, bookings, or business outcomes.

Lack of results does not qualify for a refund.

6. Payment Providers

Payments may be processed through third-party providers such as Stripe or similar platforms. Refunds, if approved, will be issued via the original payment method where possible.

7. Contact

If you believe you are eligible for a refund under this policy, please contact us through the website with your account details and reason for the request.

Need help with a refund question?

If you are unsure whether your situation falls under this policy, please contact us and include your account details and the reason for your request.

We will review eligible requests in line with this policy and respond as soon as possible.
