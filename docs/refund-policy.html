Refund Policy

Full policy details

Effective Date: 20 March 2026

1. Overview

This Refund Policy applies to vendor subscriptions purchased through Our Wedding Day Hub.

2. Setup Fee

The one-off vendor account setup fee is non-refundable in all circumstances.

3. Subscription Fees — 60 Day Guarantee

Vendor subscription fees are eligible for a refund under our 60 day satisfaction guarantee:

If you request a refund within the first 60 days of your subscription start date, we will refund up to 2 months of subscription fees.

This refund covers subscription payments only and does not apply to the one-off setup fee.

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method where possible.

4. Subscription Fees After 60 Days

Subscription fees paid outside of the 60 day guarantee window are generally non-refundable.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us through the website contact form within 60 days of your subscription start date. Please include:

  • your business name and the email address used to create your account;
  • the date you signed up; and
  • a brief note confirming you are requesting a refund under the 60 day guarantee.

6. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time by contacting us.

7. Australian Consumer Law

Nothing in this Refund Policy limits or excludes any rights you may have under the Australian Consumer Law.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or would like to request a refund, please get in touch through the contact page on the website.
