Refund Policy

This policy outlines how refunds are handled for Our Wedding Day Hub services.

Effective Date: 20 March 2026

1. Overview

This Refund Policy applies to all payments made to Our Wedding Day Hub, including vendor setup fees and subscription fees. By making a payment, you agree to this policy.

2. Vendor Setup Fee

The one-time setup fee (e.g. $50) covers the creation and activation of your vendor account, access to the platform, and the ability to build and manage your listing.

Setup fees are non-refundable once payment has been made, including if:

you change your mind;
you decide not to use the platform;
you do not complete your profile; or
you do not receive enquiries or bookings.

3. Subscription Fees

If your plan includes a recurring subscription, you will be billed according to your selected plan.

Subscription payments are generally non-refundable once charged.

You may cancel your subscription at any time, and cancellation will stop future billing, but does not refund payments already made.

4. Exceptional Circumstances

In rare cases, we may consider a refund at our discretion, including:

duplicate payments;
technical errors that prevent access to the platform; or
other situations we deem reasonable.

Any approved refunds will be assessed on a case-by-case basis and are not guaranteed.

5. No Guarantee of Results

Our Wedding Day Hub is a platform designed to increase visibility and connection opportunities. We do not guarantee enquiries, leads, bookings, or business outcomes.

As such, lack of results does not qualify for a refund.

6. Payment Providers

Payments are processed through third-party providers (such as Paddle or similar platforms). Refunds, if approved, will be issued via the original payment method where possible.

7. Contact

If you believe you are eligible for a refund under exceptional circumstances, please contact us through the website with your account details and reason for the request.
