Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use Our Wedding Day Hub.

We want our platform to feel clear, trustworthy, and respectful of your information at every step.

Effective Date: 20 March 2026

1. Overview

Our Wedding Day Hub respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose your information when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Account Information:
Name, email address, password secured through Firebase, and account type such as vendor or couple.

Vendor Information:
Business name, service categories, service locations, contact details, and any content you upload.

Couple Information:
Names, wedding date, notes, and preferences entered into your dashboard.

Usage Information:
Basic data about how you interact with the website, such as pages visited, which helps us improve the platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • create and manage your account;
  • display vendor listings publicly on the platform;
  • allow couples to find and contact vendors;
  • provide dashboard functionality;
  • improve the website and user experience; and
  • communicate with you about your account or services.

4. Vendor Listings Are Public

If you sign up as a vendor, your business information, such as name, category, service areas, and contact details, may be displayed publicly on Our Wedding Day Hub.

5. Payments

Vendor payments may be processed through third-party providers such as Stripe or other payment platforms. We do not store your full payment details.

6. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate the platform, including:

  • Firebase for authentication and database services;
  • hosting providers such as GitHub Pages or AWS; and
  • payment providers for vendor subscriptions.

These services may store or process your data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

7. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for:

  • keeping your login details secure;
  • ensuring any information you provide is accurate; and
  • only uploading content you have permission to use.

9. Access and Updates

You can update your information at any time through your dashboard. If you would like your account deleted, you may contact us and request removal.

10. Cookies and Tracking

We may use basic cookies or similar technologies to improve your browsing experience. These help us understand how users interact with the site.

11. Australian Privacy Law

We aim to comply with applicable Australian privacy laws, including the Privacy Act 1988, where applicable.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will take effect once published on this page.

13. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the website.

Questions about privacy?

If you have any questions about how your information is handled on Our Wedding Day Hub, please contact us and we’ll do our best to help.

We want the platform to feel thoughtful, transparent, and respectful of your privacy.
