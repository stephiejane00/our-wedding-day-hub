Our Promise to You

We will never sell, share, rent, trade, or pass on your personal information to any third party for marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes — ever. Not now, not in the future, not under any circumstances.

Your details belong to you. They exist solely to help us run the platform for you, and for nothing else. We do not believe in profiting from your information, and we never will.

✦ We will never sell your data. Period.
✦ Effective Date: 20 March 2026

1. Overview

Our Wedding Day Hub respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose your information when you use our website and platform.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Account Information
Name, email address, password (secured through Firebase), and account type such as vendor or couple.

Vendor Information
Business name, service categories, service locations, contact details, and any content you upload to your profile.

Couple Information
Names, wedding date, planning notes, and preferences entered into your dashboard.

Usage Information
Basic data about how you interact with the website, such as pages visited, which helps us improve the platform experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

  • create and manage your account;
  • display vendor listings publicly on the platform;
  • allow couples to find and contact vendors;
  • provide dashboard functionality;
  • improve the website and user experience; and
  • communicate with you about your account or services.

✦ We will never use your information for advertising purposes, sell it to any third party, share it with data brokers, or pass it on to anyone outside the essential services required to run this platform. This is an absolute commitment with no exceptions.

4. Vendor Listings Are Public

If you sign up as a vendor, your business information — such as your business name, category, service areas, and contact details — may be displayed publicly on Our Wedding Day Hub so that couples can discover and contact you.

5. Payments

Vendor payments are processed through trusted third-party providers such as Stripe. We do not store your full payment card details. Payment information is handled entirely by the payment provider under their own security and privacy standards.

6. Third-Party Services

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate the platform, including Firebase for authentication and database services, hosting providers for platform delivery, and payment providers for vendor subscriptions. These services are used solely to operate the platform.

✦ We do not share your personal information with advertisers, marketing platforms, data brokers, or any commercial third parties. We never have and we never will.

7. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, misuse, or disclosure. Your account data is secured through Firebase, which applies industry-standard security practices. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Responsibilities

  • keeping your login details secure;
  • ensuring any information you provide is accurate; and
  • only uploading content you have permission to use.

9. Access and Updates

You can update your personal information at any time through your dashboard. If you would like your account deleted and your data removed, please contact us and we will action your request promptly.

10. Cookies and Tracking

We may use basic cookies or similar technologies to improve your browsing experience and understand how users interact with the site. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising or third-party marketing purposes.

11. Australian Privacy Law

We aim to comply with applicable Australian privacy laws, including the Privacy Act 1988, where applicable. Our Wedding Day Hub operates from Australia under ABN 76 688 637 041.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will take effect once published on this page. We encourage you to review it periodically.

13. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us through the website. We are always happy to help and will respond as soon as possible.

✦ Questions?

Questions about your privacy?

If you have any questions about how your information is handled on Our Wedding Day Hub, please reach out and we'll do our best to help.

We want this platform to feel thoughtful, transparent, and genuinely respectful of the people who use it. Your trust means everything to us.
