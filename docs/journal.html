Planning

Tips worth keeping

01

Book your venue and celebrant first — everything else follows

Your date can't be confirmed until you have a venue, and your ceremony can't happen without a celebrant. Lock these two in early and the rest of your planning will feel infinitely clearer.

02

Your guest list is your budget — know it before you book anything

Almost every line item in a wedding scales with numbers. Knowing your rough guest count before you start approaching vendors will save you hours of recalculating quotes.

03

Send a warm message before a formal enquiry — vendors are people too

The best vendors are often fully booked through referrals and warm connections. A personal, genuine first message goes a lot further than a copy-pasted enquiry form.

04

Save your favourites as you browse — you'll thank yourself later

When you're comparing vendors weeks later, you'll wish you'd saved everyone you liked as you went. Use the save feature on every profile that catches your eye, even if you're not ready yet.
