FAQ

Common questions

Is there a free trial?

We offer a 60 day satisfaction guarantee — if you're not happy within the first 60 days, we'll refund up to 2 months of subscription fees.

What's the setup fee for?

The one-off $50 setup fee covers your profile setup and onboarding. It's a one-time cost and is non-refundable.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your profile remains active until the end of your billing period.

Are there any commissions or booking fees?

No. We never take a commission or booking fee. Every booking made through a connection from Our Wedding Day Hub is entirely yours.

Can couples use the platform for free?

Yes. Couples can browse, search, save favourites, and contact vendors completely free of charge.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards through our secure payment processor.
